# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Applies To:** All Employees

---

## Overview

This policy outlines guidelines for business expense reimbursement at Disney Corp.

## Eligible Expenses

### Travel
- **Airfare:** Economy class for flights under 6 hours, business class for longer flights
- **Hotels:** Up to $250/night in major cities, $150/night elsewhere
- **Rental Cars:** Compact or midsize vehicles only
- **Mileage:** $0.67 per mile for personal vehicle use

### Meals
- **Breakfast:** Up to $20
- **Lunch:** Up to $30
- **Dinner:** Up to $50
- **Client Entertainment:** Up to $150 per person (pre-approval required)

### Office Supplies
- Up to $100/month without approval
- Over $100 requires manager approval

### Professional Development
- Conferences: Up to $2,500/year
- Training courses: Up to $1,500/year
- Books/subscriptions: Up to $500/year

## Submission Process

1. Submit expenses within 30 days of incurrence
2. Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25
3. Use the Expense Portal at expenses.disney-corp.example
4. Approvals processed within 5 business days
5. Reimbursement paid in next payroll cycle

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- Personal entertainment
- Alcoholic beverages (except approved client dinners)
- Gym memberships
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- First-class airfare (without VP approval)

## Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Approver |
|--------|----------|
| Up to $500 | Direct Manager |
| $500 - $2,000 | Department Head |
| $2,000 - $5,000 | VP Approval |
| Over $5,000 | CFO Approval |

## Corporate Card

Employees at Manager level and above are eligible for a corporate credit card. Contact Finance at finance@disney-corp.example.
