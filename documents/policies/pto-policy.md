# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Applies To:** All Full-Time Employees

---

## Overview

Disney Corp believes in work-life balance and provides generous paid time off to all employees.

## PTO Allowances

### By Tenure

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Days |
|------------------|-----------------|
| 0-2 years | 15 days |
| 3-5 years | 20 days |
| 6-10 years | 25 days |
| 10+ years | 30 days |

### Accrual

- PTO accrues bi-weekly based on your annual allowance
- Maximum carryover: 5 days per year
- Unused PTO beyond carryover limit is forfeited on December 31st

## Requesting Time Off

1. Submit requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Manager approval required for requests over 3 consecutive days
3. Blackout periods may apply during peak business seasons

## Holidays

Disney Corp observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve

## Sick Leave

- Separate from PTO: 10 sick days per year
- No carryover for sick leave
- Doctor's note required for absences over 3 consecutive days
